Projects

DSCA: Audio Level Indicator

Mini Project for our Midterm

Our goal was to create a device that visually represents the intensity of audio signals in real-time. This project involved a combination of hardware and software components, showcasing our collaborative efforts and technical skills.
To give you a brief overview, we designed a circuit that captures audio signals and processes them to determine the amplitude. The processed data is then used to drive a set of LEDs, creating a visual representation of the audio levels. This not only provided us with a practical tool for monitoring audio, but it also served as a great learning experience in electronics, signal processing, and programming.
Throughout the project, we faced challenges and learned to troubleshoot issues together. The end result is a functional and visually appealing audio level indicator that we're proud to present. I'm looking forward to sharing more details about the technical aspects and our journey in bringing this project to life.
